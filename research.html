I'm interested in Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing and how Machine Learning can be used to solve problems from those areas.

I'm Ph.D. candidate at Instituto Superior Técnico under the supervision of Prof. Mário J. Gaspar da Silva


Thesis

Document collections hold important information expressed in natural language. Transforming text into structured data makes it possible to construct knowledge bases, which can be explored through queries and data analyses.

My research focuses on how to extract information from document collections (i.e, news archives, scientific articles, etc.). Concretely, on how extract named-entities (i.e, persons, organizations, locations, etc.) and relationships among them.

Within the context of my thesis I have explored supervised, distantly supervised and semi-supervised bootstrapping methods for semantic relationship extraction.
